# 🚀 Beyond Life — Secure Digital Will Management System

## 🧠 Overview
Beyond Life is a secure web-based platform that allows users to create, manage, and securely share digital wills.

It focuses on **privacy, encryption, and controlled access**, ensuring that sensitive data is only accessible to the right people at the right time.

---

## 💡 Problem
In today’s digital world, people store important information like:
- Passwords  
- Bank details  
- Legal documents  

But there is no secure way to share this data after death.

Traditional systems:
- ❌ Lack security  
- ❌ No structured access  
- ❌ Risk of misuse  

---

## ✅ Solution
Beyond Life provides:
- 🔐 Encrypted digital will storage  
- 🧠 Role-based access control (family, lawyer, etc.)  
- ⚙️ Automated access after verification  
- 📄 Secure document handling  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

**Frontend**
- React.js  

**Backend**
- Node.js  
- Express.js  

**Database**
- MongoDB  

**Security**
- AES-256 Encryption  
- JWT Authentication  
- Attribute-Based Access Control  

---

## 🔄 How It Works

1. User creates a digital will  
2. Data is encrypted  
3. Beneficiaries are assigned  
4. Executor verifies death certificate  
5. Access is granted to authorized users
